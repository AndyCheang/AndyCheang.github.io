I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at Singapore Management University, where I am supervised by [Prof. Yang Deng](https://dengyang17.github.io/). My current research interests include LLM factuality, reliability, and hallucination detection.
Prior to this, I was a master’s student at the University of Macau under the supervision of [Prof. Derek Wong](https://www.fst.um.edu.mo/personal/derek-wong/).

## Research Interests

- My research focuses on **knowledge utilization in large language models**. I study how **LLMs recall parametric knowledge** through their *internal representations* and *behavior*, with the goal of understanding **when and why they hallucinate**. I am also interested in **retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)**, particularly in improving how LLMs leverage **external information** effectively and faithfully.
